# Use of Funds

For a 25,000 USD grant, funds would be used to turn the current public prototype into a more complete open-source benchmark and toolkit.

## Allocation

- 40% engineering time for evaluator, adapters, and CI templates
- 25% benchmark curation and real-world trace collection
- 15% documentation and tutorials for solo builders
- 10% web3/infrastructure safety rule packs
- 10% community feedback, maintenance, and release work

## What the grant unlocks

Without funding, this remains a side project. With funding, the work can become a usable public good with stronger examples, better docs, and enough benchmark coverage for other builders to trust and extend it.
